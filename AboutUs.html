ABOUT US

Based in the beautiful river town of Whanganui, My Name is proud to offer a thoughtfully curated range of gifts, personalised treasures, and New Zealand souvenirs. We believe every gift should feel special, whether it’s something made just for you or a keepsake that captures the spirit of Aotearoa.

Our online store makes it simple to browse and shop from anywhere, giving you a stress-free way to find the perfect present. If you’re local or visiting Whanganui, we’d love to welcome you in person at our shop on Victoria Avenue, where you can explore our full collection.

Whether you’re after a unique memento, a customised piece, or something to brighten a loved one’s day, My Name is here to make gifting effortless and meaningful.
